Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical session take?

Our standard sessions last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfits while ensuring we capture your best angles and expressions.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide detailed guidance upon booking.

How many photos will I receive?

You'll receive access to a private online gallery with 20-30 professionally edited photos to choose from, with options to purchase additional images.

Do you provide hair and makeup services?

Yes, we can arrange professional hair and makeup services for an additional fee. This service should be booked at least 48 hours in advance of your session.

What is your rescheduling policy?

We understand that plans change. You can reschedule your session with 48 hours notice at no additional charge. Last-minute changes may incur a rebooking fee.
